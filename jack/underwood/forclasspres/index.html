About the Candidate

“Jack Underwood is an ideal candidate for the position of 9th grade President due to his exemplary leadership skills and genuine commitment to fostering a positive school environment. Throughout his time at our school, Jack has consistently demonstrated his ability to bring people together, listen to diverse perspectives, and drive collaborative initiatives. His approachable and friendly demeanor makes him approachable to all students, ensuring that their voices are heard and valued. Jack's previous involvement in various extracurricular activities showcases his dedication to the school community, and his innovative ideas for fundraisers, events, and improvements reflect his passion for enhancing the overall student experience. With a proven track record of responsibility and determination, Jack is well-equipped to represent the interests of the 9th grade class and address any concerns that may arise. By electing Jack Underwood as 9th grade President, students can be confident in his ability to lead with integrity, empathy, and a genuine desire to make our school an even better place for everyone.”

Q & A with Jack

  • What's your plan to fight ever increasing campus shop prices?

    Turn them back to a fair deal.

  • What’s the reason you're running for class president in six words?

    To be a voice for freshmen.

  • Why vote for you instead of someone else?

    Because I really do care.
